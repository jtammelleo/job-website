General Information

For more than 100 years, oil and gas workers have laid the foundation for California’s vibrant economy; their remarkable grit and dedication has served as the catalyst for the state’s unprecedented growth and unparalleled prosperity. However, as market forces transition the state towards clean energy sources, California must not leave these workers behind.

This website serves as a resource for oil and gas workers of all types–from non-union to union, blue collar to white collar, entry-level to CEO–who seek new employment opportunities with a similar skill set, comparable pay, and are located nearby. This website will also factor options for workers who seek to retire. Below are resources for various types of workers.
